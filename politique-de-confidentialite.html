Privacy Policy — Color Takeover

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Color Takeover ("the Game") is a free strategy game developed by Darkvenox, available on browser and Android. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

Color Takeover does not collect any personal data. The game runs entirely in your browser and on your device.

The only data stored is your game progress (levels completed, trophies, settings), which is saved locally on your device using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.

3. No Ads

The game contains no advertisements and does not use any advertising networks.

4. No Third-Party Services

The game does not use any analytics, tracking, or third-party services that collect data.

5. Children's Privacy

Color Takeover does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The game is safe for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
